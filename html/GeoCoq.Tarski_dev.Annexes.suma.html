Definition of the sum of angles. SumA A B C D E F G H I means that ABC+DEF = GHI.

Definition SumA A B C D E F G H I:=
  ∃ J, CongA C B J D E F ∧ ¬ OS B C A J ∧ CongA A B J G H I.

The Isi predicate describes the fact that the sum of the two angles is "interior", i.e doesn't exceed a flat angle.

Definition Isi A B C D E F:=
  A≠B ∧ (Out E D F ∨ ¬ Bet A B C) ∧
  ∃ J, CongA C B J D E F ∧ ¬ OS B C A J ∧ ¬ TS A B C J.
